Meet Amy and Steve

My husband and I have enjoyed four dogs in our 32+ year marriage. For 24 of these years, we were blessed to have two under foot at the same time. Sandie and Sam were our first two kids - Golden Retrievers whom we loved on until old age separated us. After five weeks of an empty household (well, 3 two-footed toddlers had come along, so not really “empty”!), we added another Golden, Ben. When Ben was 1.5 years, we adopted our first Yellow lab, Charleston. We pretty much raised our kids with these two loves. In Jan '19, our last one crossed the Rainbow bridge.

With two kids in college (and a high schooler), we want to love on dogs but cannot commit to full time at the moment. We want to, trust me! But, we also want to enjoy a few weekends away every now and then. So, we’re thinking that loving on your loves will give us the freedom and fun that we’re craving! We’ve got a six foot fence and we’re used to runners (the lab gave us that experience, thank you. Lol) so we know how to keep doors closed and bolted. Dogs are us!
